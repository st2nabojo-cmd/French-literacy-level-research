## Description

Elsie Rockwell is a researcher at the Center for Research and Advanced Studies (CINVESTAV) in Mexico City. She is a leading figure in Latin American educational research, particularly on literacy and schooling from an ethnographic and historical perspective.

## Role in Research Domain

Co-author (with [[Ana Maria Galvão]]) of the essay [[2026-04-22-paradoxes-french-literacy-instruction]], which bridges French and Anglo-American literacy scholarship. Her perspective as a Latin American researcher provides a useful external vantage point on French literacy traditions.
