## Description

Aude Thomas is a researcher in the Department of Psychology at [[Université de Lorraine]], Nancy, France. She is the lead author of the paper introducing the [[EPLE Scale]] (Échelle Préscolaire de Littératie Émergente), the first validated French-language tool for assessing early literacy in preschool children.
