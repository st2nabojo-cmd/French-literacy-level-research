## Description

The Université de Lorraine is a French public research university based in Nancy and Metz, France. It is home to a Department of Psychology and the LISEC research laboratory (Laboratoire Interuniversitaire des Sciences de l'Éducation et de la Communication), both of which contributed to the EPLE literacy research.

## Role in Research Domain

The institution behind the [[EPLE Scale]] study — all seven co-authors of [[2026-04-22-eple-french-literacy-scale]] are affiliated with its psychology and education departments.
